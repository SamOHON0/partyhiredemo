Who

S&G Party Hire supplies party furniture and portaloo hire anywhere in Ireland. Professional equipment, reliable delivery, fair pricing. That's the whole idea.

We supply everything you need to run a great event. Tables, chairs, bars, dance floors, linens, portaloos, luxury trailer units, and all the extras. Delivered, set up, and collected.

We deliver anywhere in Ireland. Whether it's a garden party for 30 or a marquee wedding for 300, we have the stock and the logistics to make it work.

Our approach is simple: answer the phone, show up on time, leave the site cleaner than we found it. No fuss, no excuses.
